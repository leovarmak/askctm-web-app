The Central A/C Seminar hall is the venue for all the important events at ASK College of Technology and Management. The year 2010 this seminar hall established its presence and value for the Institute by holding many events and functions. It was inaugurated by the Chief Guest of the Konathala Lakshmi Narayana (Pedababu) gaaru.

Air-conditioned seminar halls with a minimum of 150 seating capacity each are available in the campus. They are well equipped with audio and visual systems. Events such as small meetings and intra department level seminars/symposia, etc are organized here.
